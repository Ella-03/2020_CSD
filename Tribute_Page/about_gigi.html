Growning Up

Gigi was born in Budapest, Hungary in 1936. Her mother was German and her father was Bulgarian.
As a child, Gigi was good friends with a girl next door. They would hangout around town, have parties at their houses, and do other fun activities.

WWII

When the war started, Gigi's father knew that she would no longer be able to see her neighbor friend. Since her father was an American Translator,
he also knew that his family would have to leave the country.

Halfway through WWII, Gigi and her family fled the country by boat and started towards America. Sadly before leaving, she would experience horrors that
no one should have to go through. Gigi's friend was shot since she was Jewish and one of the main bridges, that was located in front of her home, was bombed.

Presently

Gigi is currently 84 years young. She is married to Miff, who owns a furniture repair shop. Gigi has three kids and is a Grandmother to five and a
Great Grandmother to two.
